Commonly Asked Questions.

Locker Assignment

Why can't I sign in to the website?

If you are in 9th grade, please sign in to the website using your middle school email. Please keep in mind that you will receive your assignment at your new DVHS email (ID#@students.srvusd.net). If the issue persists or if you are not a 9th grader, please contact the administrator mentioned in the email sent by your school.

How does it work?

While registration is open, you will input your preferences on this website. After registration closes, locker assignments are done on a first-come-first-serve basis keeping your preferences in mind.

When will I know my assigned locker?

An email with your final assignment will be sent to your school email address (ID#@students.srvusd.net) after registration closes.

What if I want to change my locker preferences?

While registration is open, you can change your preferences anytime by signing in, updating your preferences, and clicking "Update". However, as assignments are done on a first-come-first-serve basis, this will move you to the back of the line.

What if I want to change my locker/partner AFTER the assignment?

Requests for changes cannot be addressed through the online assignment procedure. If you have any special requirements, please contact the administrator mentioned in the email sent by your school.

What if I have special requirements for my locker assignment?

Special accommodations cannot be addressed through the online assignment procedure. If you have any physical/medical constraints, please contact the administrator mentioned in the email sent by your school.
